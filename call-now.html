FAQs

How quickly can someone reach me? ⌄

It depends on operator availability and traffic. Call and we’ll give you the best available ETA for your location.

Do you run 24/7? ⌄

Yes — 24/7, subject to operator availability.

Are you the recovery company? ⌄

No. Recovero is an introducer/dispatch service. We connect you to an available independent operator who attends and provides the service.

How is the price worked out? ⌄

Pricing typically depends on your location, vehicle type, conditions, and destination distance. The operator confirms price before dispatch where possible.

Who do I pay? ⌄

Payment is arranged directly with the attending independent operator.

Motorway / hard shoulder safety ⌄

If you’re in immediate danger, call 999 first. If it’s safe, move to a safer location and then call us for an ETA + price.
