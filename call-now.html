FAQs

What if I’m on a motorway or hard shoulder?

If you’re in immediate danger, call 999 first. For motorway or hard shoulder incidents, follow safety guidance and then call us so we can connect you to an available operator.

How fast can you get to me?

ETA varies with location, traffic and local operator availability. We connect you to the nearest available independent operator and they confirm ETA directly.

Do you cover nights and weekends?

Yes, dispatch requests are handled 24/7 including nights, weekends and bank holidays, subject to operator availability.

Do you recover vans and EVs?

Many local operators can assist with vans and EVs depending on vehicle size, condition and equipment availability. Tell us your vehicle details when you call.

How do payments work?

Payment is taken directly by the attending independent operator.
