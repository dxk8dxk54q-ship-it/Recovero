FAQs

Are you the recovery company?

Recovero arranges your booking and connects you with a local recovery operator who attends the job.

Who do I pay?

You pay the attending operator directly when the job is complete.

How is the price calculated?

Pricing depends on distance, vehicle type, access, time of day, and the situation. You’ll get price confirmation as early as possible.

What areas do you cover?

Portsmouth and surrounding areas (including Havant, Fareham, Gosport and nearby). Longer-distance transport is available on request.

How quickly can you attend?

Arrival times vary with traffic and availability. We prioritise the nearest suitable recovery partner and share an ETA as soon as it’s confirmed.

What information do you need from me?

Pickup location/postcode, destination (if needed), vehicle make/model, and what’s happened (breakdown/accident/non-runner).

What if I need to cancel?

Tell us as soon as possible. If a recovery vehicle is already on the way or has arrived, a callout charge may apply.

What if I’m on a motorway / in a dangerous location?

If you’re in immediate danger, call 999 first. For motorways or hard shoulder breakdowns, follow safety guidance and we’ll prioritise urgent roadside help.
