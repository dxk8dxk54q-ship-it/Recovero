FAQs

Are you the recovery company?

No. Recovero is a booking and coordination service. We connect you with an independent local recovery operator who attends.

Who do I pay?

You pay the attending operator directly on completion. Recovero does not take payment from customers.

How is the price calculated?

Pricing depends on distance, vehicle type, access, time of day, and the situation. Operator confirms price before dispatch where possible.

What areas do you cover?

Portsmouth and surrounding areas (including Havant, Fareham, Gosport and nearby). Longer-distance transport is available on request.

How quickly can you attend?

Arrival times vary based on traffic and operator availability. We’ll connect you to the nearest available operator and they’ll confirm an ETA.

What information do you need from me?

Pickup location/postcode, destination (if needed), vehicle make/model, and what’s happened (breakdown/accident/non-runner).

What if I need to cancel?

Tell us as soon as possible. If an operator has already been dispatched or attended, you may be charged by the operator for time/travel.

What if I’m on a motorway / in a dangerous location?

If you’re in immediate danger, call 999. For motorways/hard shoulder, follow safety guidance and we’ll prioritise connecting you to an available operator.
