FAQs

How fast can you get to me in Fareham?
Response time depends on operator availability, traffic, and exact location in or around Fareham. We connect you to an available local independent operator and share the ETA they provide.
Do you cover nights and weekends?
Yes, we take dispatch requests 24/7, including evenings and weekends, subject to independent operator availability at the time of your call.
What information do you need before dispatch?
Your location or postcode, vehicle type, what has happened, passenger details, and destination if transport is needed. This helps us match the right operator.
Do you recover EVs and vans?
We can arrange EV and van recovery where an available independent operator has suitable equipment and capacity. Tell us the vehicle size and issue so we can check options quickly.
What if I break down on a motorway or hard shoulder?
Your safety comes first: follow motorway safety guidance, exit the vehicle from the passenger side when safe, stand behind barriers where possible, and call emergency services if there is immediate danger. We will then connect you to an available operator.
How do payments work?
Recovero is a dispatch/introducer service. The attending independent operator completes the job and takes payment directly from you.
